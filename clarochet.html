About

About our Business

At Clarochet, we specialize in creating one-of-a-kind crochet pieces that are both beautiful and functional. Our business was founded by Clara Estephan, who has been passionate about crochet since she was a child. After years of creating pieces for family and friends, Clara Estephan decided to turn her hobby into a business.

Our Mission

Our mission is to bring joy and beauty to people's lives through the art of crochet. We believe that handmade items have a special energy and soul that cannot be replicated by mass-produced products. We are committed to using high-quality materials and creating pieces that are not only aesthetically pleasing, but also functional and durable.

Our Values

At Clarochet, we value creativity, quality, and sustainability. We strive to create pieces that are not only beautiful, but also environmentally friendly. We use natural fibers whenever possible and avoid materials that are harmful to the environment.

Our Process

Our creative process begins with selecting the best materials for each project. We carefully choose fibers that are soft, durable, and easy to work with. From there, we use a variety of crochet techniques to bring our ideas to life. Each piece is made with great care and attention to detail.

Our Inspiration

We draw inspiration from many sources, including nature, art, and our customers. We love collaborating with clients to create custom pieces that are tailored to their individual needs and preferences.
